My Childhood

I was an anxious, inquisitve, rebellious little boy when I used to live
in a small town called 'Pharsatikar', situated in southern part of Nepal.
My most pleasant memories are of playing with my childhood friends with a
carefree attitude I had back then. Not only was I oblivious to the outside
world, I didn't even care to know about my locality. I just used to roam
here and there, play all day and night and occasionally pick fights even
with people bigger and stronger than me.
